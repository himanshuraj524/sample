Education gives us a knowledge of the world around us and changes it into something better. It develops in us a perspective of looking at life. Why Education is important in our life. Having education in an area helps people think, feel, and behave in a way that contributes to their success, and improves not only their personal satisfaction but also their community. In addition, education develops human personality, thoughts, dealing with others and prepares people for life experiences.

What is E-learning. E-education or e-learning is the delivery of education or any type of training by electronic teaching methods. This electronic method can be a computer or a smartphone where teaching material is accessed by use of the internet usually. E-learning has several benefits over traditional modes of learning in India.
